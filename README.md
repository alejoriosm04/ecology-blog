# Images Directory

This directory is for your personal photographs that serve as evidence for your ecology experience log.

## Required Images:

### Outdoor Activities (2 required):
- `outdoor1.jpg` - Evidence of river cleanup/ecosystem restoration activity
- `outdoor2.jpg` - Evidence of tree planting/urban reforestation project

### Classroom Activities (3 required):
- `class1.jpg` - Evidence of composting workshop materials and process
- `class2.jpg` - Evidence of carbon footprint calculation worksheets
- `class3.jpg` - Evidence of zero waste challenge materials and alternatives

## Photo Guidelines:

1. **All photos must be your own** (as required by your teacher)
2. **Show clear evidence** of the activity described
3. **Include yourself or your work** when possible to prove participation
4. **Ensure good lighting and clarity** for professional presentation
5. **Consider privacy** - avoid showing other students' faces without permission

## Recommended Photo Content:

### For Outdoor Activities:
- Before/after shots of cleanup areas
- You working with tools or materials
- Collected waste or planted trees
- Group shots of collaborative work
- Environmental conditions during activity

### For Classroom Activities:
- Your worksheets or calculations
- Materials used in experiments
- Your hands working with composting materials
- Zero waste products you tested
- Workshop setup and your participation

## File Format:
- Use `.jpg` or `.png` format
- Keep file sizes reasonable (under 2MB each)
- Use descriptive filenames as listed above

Replace the placeholder images in the HTML with your actual photos once you have them!
